About Diamond Tech

Our business model is to partner with global ICT brands since 2018. Founded by Fortune Makoni, we are a proudly youth-owned company. Our company, incorporated in Zimbabwe and South Africa, specializes in information and communication technology (ICT). Our primary areas of expertise encompass hardware, software, consumables, and electrical components.
